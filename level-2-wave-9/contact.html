GROUP RESERVATIONS

Reservations are needed for groups of 7 or more.

Reservations can only be made during off-peak hours and generally require 2-weeks notice. We will help you get the most out of your visit with hired staff to help with check-in, orientations and belaying.

IMPORTANT: The Source is unable to accommodate groups of 7 or more without a reservation. Groups of 7 or more might be turned away during peak hours. Calling ahead or booking online is crucial to avoid being denied entry. Call us at 360-694-9096 for pricing and scheduling.

Best time to climb

Peak time change throughout the year. Still, there are a few regular program times you may want to avoid for faster check-in. For example, the mezzanine is unavailable during birthday parties on Saturdays 3:30-5pm and Sundays from 1-3pm, and after-school programs from 4:30-6:30 Mondays through Fridays during the school year.
